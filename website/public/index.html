Sass Conf

October 2013

New York City

Come get Sassy this October in New York City! Introducing SassConf, a two day conference devoted to modern front-end development. Prepare yourself for a fantastic two day conference; day one will be filled with talks from big players in the Sass and Front-End communities and day two will be an unconference where you will be able to break out into smaller groups to talk and work. Leave us your email and you'll be the first to know when tickets are available and speakers are announced. Hope to see you there!
