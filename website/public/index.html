Sass Conf

October 12 to 13

Schoolastic Auditorum, New York City

Come get Sassy this October in New York City! Introducing SassConf, a two day conference devoted to modern front-end development. Prepare yourself for a fantastic two day conference; day one will be filled with talks from big players in the Sass and Front-End communities and day two will be an unconference where you will be able to break out into smaller groups to talk and work. Leave us your email and you'll be the first to know when tickets are available and speakers are announced. Hope to see you there!


Keynotes

We are ecstatic to announce our two keynote speakers! Chris Eppstein, Staff Engineer for LinkedIn, creator of Compas, and maintainer of Sass, will be keynoting our first day. Hampton Catlin, Founder of Moovweb and inventor of Sass, Haml, and m.wikipedia.org, will be keynoting our second day.

  • Chris Eppstein

    Staff Engineer, LinkedIn

    It Takes a Village to Raise a Website

    Chris will talk about the guiding principles that have gotten Sass to where it is today, the vision for where it is going, and how we can't get there without amazing people like you. Together we can make the the entire web a better place for users, designers and developers.

  • Hampton Catlin

    Founder, Moovweb

    The Front End Revolution: A Call to Arms

    Developing for the front end is fundamentally different than the backend and Let's talk an entirely new approach to the problems we face on the front end, problems which are distinct than the backend solutions we have grown used to. Traditionally minded programmers welcome, but this talk is a siren song for those whose main weapons are HTML, Sass and maybe a touch of Javascript. This is a declaration of independence from the backend.
